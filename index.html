Research

I’m interested in machine learning systems that not only predict outcomes but also reliably integrate into human decision-making in settings such as healthcare, education, and online platforms. I aim to develop human-centered ML methods and evaluation frameworks for high-stakes health and social systems that make AI-supported decisions auditable, robust to distribution shift, and suitable for real-world deployment Representative papers are highlighted.
